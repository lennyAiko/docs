PROGRAMS IN KWASU

  • Regular
  • PDP
  • IDEL

REGULAR

Regular is the usual b.Sc undergraduate degree. The tuition fee is said to be 210,000 naira. Regular students on have two semesters. You can apply for regular through normal 100 level entry or through direct entry.

PDP

PDP stands for Professional degree program. They usually have three semesters. Their school fees is more expensive than regular, it is said to be around 250,000 naira.

IDEL

IDEL stands for Institute for distance and e-learning. They are almost like regular students but with fewer classes. Their school fees is said to be around 150,000 naira.
